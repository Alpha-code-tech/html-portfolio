The Best Movies According to Shalom

My top 3 movies of all-time.

F1

This is about formula 1. It talks about determination and the pursuit of excellence in a high-speed world.

Oppenheimer

Talks about the efforts put into the development of the atomic bomb and its implications.

Mountain Queen

Talks about the struggles and triumphs of a young girl in a remote village as she pursues her dream of becoming a mountain climber.
